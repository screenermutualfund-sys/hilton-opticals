About Us

Hyderabad's Fastest-Growing & Most Beloved Optical Store

Founded in 2023, Hilton Opticals has rapidly become one of Hyderabad's most trusted names in premium eyewear. Located in the bustling Sultan Bazaar — the heart of the city — we bring world-class optical solutions right to your doorstep.

With over 5,000+ satisfied customers and a perfect 5/5 Google Rating, we are India's highest rated optical store. Our mission is simple: provide the widest range of frames, lenses, sunglasses, smartglasses, and accessories for men, women, kids, and everyone — at lasting value.
